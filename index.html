about

Udayan is a San Diego- and Carbon-based, aspiring front-end web developer, who dabbles in standup and writing. You may have heard of a little novel called "The Stranger." Well he didn't write that one, Camus did, but he's read it. His hope is to put his own works out there soon, be they written, drawn or programmed.

He (Udayan, not Camus) loves to learn - whether it's the newest skills in biotechnology or his newest passion for learning front-end web development. He believes education is an ongoing journey, empowering individuals to take part in a larger conversation and express different facets of themselves.

He has a degree in Biology and loves a good read. Both Darwin and Shakespeare are his patron saints. He also updates a webcomic here as often as he can, which draws from his experiences and reflections.

Look on his works, ye Mighty, and despair. Or, just kind of go "heh, nice."
